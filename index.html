Professional Experience

Over the past 1.6 years, I have contributed to a startup, creating a system now serving over 20,000 users across Kochi, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, and Chennai. My role in enhancing dashboard functionalities significantly boosted our team's productivity by 40%.
